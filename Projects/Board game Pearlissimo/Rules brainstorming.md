Balance axioms: 

**Colors themes: **

All the cards are themed based on their color value.

- Rainbow, powerful but expensive effects, 
    - Cost: Often “Any Color” or “Same Color”
    - Usage: General powerful effects, leveraging the value, Getting the Great Unicorn parts
- Red: Sabotage / stealing, more color centered costs and play well with criminal stacks / Mastermind, usually high bluff count.
    - Cost: Specific colors and high bluff count. Criminal Stacks and Mastermind Requirements
    - Usage: General powerful effects, leveraging the value, Getting the Great Unicorn parts.  Lot of Illegal cards.
- Blue: permanent cards, always active, fairly global costs, high
    - Cost: .Stars Business Stacks and CEO Requirements
    - Usage: General powerful effects, leveraging the value, Getting the Great Unicorn parts
- Green : good guys, playing with business stacks, CEO bonuses, usually Business stacks costs and requirements. Lots of secret cards.
    - Cost: .Stars Business Stacks and CEO Requirements
    - Usage: General powerful effects, leveraging the value, Getting the Great Unicorn parts
- Yellow: easy resources, some free stuff, banking. Cost a lot of stars
    - Cost: .Stars Business Stacks and CEO Requirements
    - Usage: General powerful effects, leveraging the value, redeming victory cards
- White: drawing more cards, information fishing, usually cost more stars than colors, fairly high bluff count. Fair amount of Illegal cards.
    - Cost: .Stars Business Stacks and CEO Requirements
    - Usage: General powerful effects, leveraging the value, Getting the Great Unicorn parts

All colors must have the same amount of cards, except for rainbow which should have a little less.

Cards may also have modifiers, additionnal effects or aspects which are usually situational and positive for the player.
